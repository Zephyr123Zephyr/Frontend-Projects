COME AND PLAY AOE3 TOGETHER!

I am General Zephyr, a ten-year veteran player of AOE3. I invite you to enjoy this competitive game filled with a sense of history and artistic flair. Download from Steam...

THIS IS AGE OF EMPIRES 3

Embark on an exploration of the New World map, engage in battles as your favorite civilization, and experience the unfolding of history from the medieval era to modernization.

VIVID AND DETAILED GRAPHIC

As a real-time strategy game, you can see the details even at close distances. Unique smoke, explosion, and water ripple effects, along with physical trajectories, perfectly recreate the scenes.

REPRODUCED HISTORY OF CIVILIZATIONS

The dev team conducts in-depth investigations into the histories of different civilizations, creating completely different economic pathways and combat styles. Furthermore, there are either fictional or real-happened battles for you to experience.

UNIQUE SHIPMENT MECHANIC

Unlike typical RTS games, the battle in AOE3 begins even before the game starts. As the commander of a colony, your home city will provide you with crucial shipping support based on a pre-set deck of cards, staging a war beyond the battlefield!

HOME CITY CUSTOMIZATION

With the increase in the number of battles, your experience points can be used to embellish your home city. Architecture, residents, weather, decorations, and holiday celebrations - nothing can’t be added! It’s extraordinarily lively!
