Below is a list of some of my favorite units in the game. However, there are hundreds of more content for you to experience. Click on their introduction for more info.

------------ Cavalry ------------

HUSSAR
Unit Information
Type Heavy Cavalry
Civilization Most European
Attack Melee
Siege
Overview
Hussars are the basic cavalry for most of the European civilizations. They have the most hit points of all basic cavalry, but otherwise lack distinguishing characteristics.
CUIRASSIER
Unit Information
Type Heavy Cavalry
Civilization French
Attack Melee Splash: 2
Siege
Overview
Cuirassiers can only attack in melee combat, but deal splash damage, which makes them extremely effective against large armies but are still vulnerable to heavy infantry and ranged cavalry.
AXE RIDER
Unit Information
Type Heavy Cavalry
Civilization Lakota
Attack Melee
Siege
Overview
The Axe Rider has a higher attack than most other cavalry units, making it quick and efficient at killing Shock Infantry. However, Axe Riders also have relatively low hit points (before upgrades), so it is recommended to guard them from heavy infantry and ranged cavalry.
IRON FLAIL
Unit Information
Type Heavy Cavalry
Civilization Chinese
Attack Melee Splash: 1
Siege
Overview
The Iron Flail is a powerful heavy cavalry available to the Chinese. If Double Faced Armor is shipped, it boosts the range resist up to an impressive 41%, allowing it to tank an incredible amount of ranged fire.

------------ Infantry ------------

MUSKETEER
Unit Information
Type Heavy Infantry
Civilization Most European
Attack Range
Melee
Siege
Overview
Musketeers are simple to mass produce due to their low coin cost and are cheaper than more specialised units such as Skirmishers or Halberdiers. Therefore, they can be powerful generally, especially in vast quantities.
ASHIGARU
Unit Information
Type Heavy Infantry
Civilization Japanese
Attack Range
Melee
Siege
Overview
Ashigaru Musketeers are a more expensive version of the regular Musketeer, with slightly more hit points, speed and ranged attack, but with noticeably less damage vs units in melee, including cavalry and shock infantry.
JAGUAR PROWL KNIGHT
Unit Information
Type Heavy Infantry
Civilization Aztecs
Attack Melee Multiple: 3
Siege
Overview
The Jaguar Prowl Knight is a very powerful and fast infantry unit of the Aztec civilization with a lot of hit points and attack and is very effective against heavy infantry and heavy cavalry units.
PISTOLERO
Unit Information
Type Heavy Infantry
Civilization Mercenaries
Attack Range
Melee
Siege
Overview
The Pistolero is an Outlaw unit armed with a pistol. As with other outlaws they cost little coin but many population slots. Compared to standard Musketeers, Pistoleros are faster and slightly more powerful.

------------ Artillery ------------

FALCONET
Unit Information
Type Artillery
Civilization Most European
Attack Siege(Bombard)
Overview
The Falconet is a simple yet surprisingly useful and capable artillery piece, which is important because of their early accessibility and impact on early battles, due to its bonus against infantry that combines well with its area damage and versatility.
GREAT BOMBARD
Unit Information
Type Artillery
Civilization Ottomans
Attack Siege(Bombard)
Overview
The Great Bombard has a giant damage output, shredding any infantry it encounters on the way with just two of them. The Great Bombard is also excellent at taking out enemy buildings with this extremely high damage.
FLYING CROW
Unit Information
Type Artillery
Civilization Chinese
Attack Siege(Bombard)
Overview
Flying Crows can be fielded very early in the game if the Confucian Academy is built to advance into the Commerce Age. It sends a gunpowder-powered missile effective against units and buildings.
GATLING GUN
Unit Information
Type Artillery
Civilization United States
Attack Siege Rapid
Overview
The Gatling Gun is unusual compared to the other artillery pieces in the game. While devastating against infantry they are less effective against cavalry and buildings, doing reduced damage against them.

------------ Marine ------------

GALLEON
Unit Information
Type Warship
Civilization Most European
Attack Siege(Bombard)
Overview
The Galleon is a fast and powerful military main battle ship that is capable of training infantry and cavalry directly onto foreign soil, acting as a mobile Barracks and Stables.
FUCHUAN
Unit Information
Type Warship
Civilization Chinese
Attack Siege(bombard)
Overview
With 2,500 hit points, the Fuchuan is the strongest easily acquired naval unit in Age of Empires III. But Fuchuan has a build limit of two, instead of the usual three for a Frigate, and is incredibly expensive.
TLALOC CANOE
Unit Information
Type War Canoe
Civilization Aztecs
Attack Siege(Flaming Arrow)
Overview
Tlaloc Canoe is very effective against other boats and undefended buildings. It has good range, speed, and damage, but like all canoes, is most potent if built in numbers.
IRONCLAD
Unit Information
Type Warship
Civilization United States
Mexicans
Attack Siege(Bombard)
Overview
Ironclads are a faster and swifter firing version of a Monitor with diminished range, line of sight and hit points (versus a fully upgraded Monitor). Although it looks very tough, but it's rather slow than most warships.
